Good Trouble

Franklin Mayoral Race 2023

Meredith was recently featured by local TN media outlets after publicly speaking out against controversial Franklin At-Large Alderman, and current mayoral candidate, Gabrielle Hanson. Hanson, a notorious MAGA supporter, has built quite a reputation for herself in 2023, including voting to deny a permit for the Franklin Pride Festival to take place, threatening the Nashville Airport board in July, and falsely claiming she predicted and tried to warn police about the Covenant School mass shooting earlier this March.‍

Recently, she has gained even more attention, including nationally, as new evidence is uncovered and the laundry list of issues concerning her problematic past continues to grow.

Meredith Dunn and Joshua Patrick of WCYD, and activist Katie Parham, silently mock Gabrielle Hanson at the Franklin BOMA meeting on Sep. 26 with signs and shirts mentioning several of Gabrielle Hanson's known aliases used while promoting prostitution in the 1990s.
Image by Coleman Bomar, Williamson Herald

On September 26, Meredith and fellow WCYD leader Joshua Patrick attended the Franklin BOMA meeting to peacefully protest and call for the censure of Gabrielle Hanson after it was revealed Hanson was previously arrested for promoting prostitution in the mid-1990s while living in Dallas, Texas. Despite this new evidence and significant public support in favor of taking action against Hanson, the board ultimately decided not to censure her.

"I continue to be infuriated by the cowardice of the board and taking inaction against Alderman Hanson. Their decision tonight represented nothing but kicking the can down the line until after the election that is coming up…There's no action for the people of Franklin and what she's done.”
-Meredith
