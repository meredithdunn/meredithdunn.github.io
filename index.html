MEREDITH

About Meredith

Meredith is a passionate and dedicated Nashville-based political activist. With a relentless commitment to fostering positive change in her community, she continues to make a lasting impact on local politics through her advocacy and campaign work.

She is currently finishing her degree in Political Science at Middle Tennessee State University.

Local Leadership

As the President of Williamson County Young Democrats (WCYD), Meredith has played a pivotal role in mobilizing and empowering the next generation of progressive leaders and voters in Tennessee. Under her leadership, the organization continues to be a dynamic force in Williamson County, promoting young voter registration, and advocating for policies that prioritize social justice and inclusive representation.

With her dynamic leadership, relentless passion for change, and remarkable campaign expertise, Meredith continues to be a driving force in the Nashville political scene, working to create a more inclusive, equitable, and progressive future for Tennessee and its residents.
