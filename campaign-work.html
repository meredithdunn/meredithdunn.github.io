Nashville Mayor

Meredith's dedication to progressive values was prominently displayed during her pivotal role in Nashville Mayor Freddie O'Connell's recent 2023 campaign.

As a key campaign worker, she worked tirelessly to support O'Connell's vision for the city. However, her journey began with her work for candidate Jim Gingrich, who now serves on Freddie O'Connell's mayoral transition team.

Throughout both campaigns, she demonstrated her strategic insight, grassroots organizing, and unparalleled energy, contributing significantly to O'Connell's successful bid for mayor.

Tennessee Governor Samp;
5th Congressional District U.S House Representative

Meredith's political journey began in 2022 through her simultaneous involvement in two major TN races:

Dr. Jason Martin's campaign challenging incumbent Bill Lee for Tennessee Governor, and Senator Heidi Campbell's campaign against Andy Ogles for TN District 5 U.S. House Representative.

While the results of these campaigns did not yield the desired outcomes, the evident frustration felt by many TN voters was undeniable. Since then, there has been a resounding call for progressive change throughout the state.
